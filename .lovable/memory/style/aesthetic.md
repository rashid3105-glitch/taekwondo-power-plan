One palette everywhere — no dark/light split between logged-out and logged-in.

Base: light neutral background (210 30% 98%), white cards, dark navy text (222 35% 12%).
Primary: electric blue `222 95% 56%` — used for CTAs, links, active states, primary tab.
Accent: lime `80 90% 50%` — used for highlights, success states, energy moments.
Speed: cyan `190 95% 48%`. Explosive: red `350 90% 56%`. Power: lime.

No pastel washes. High contrast, vivid, professional — sport-tech feel.
Landing tokens (`--landing-navy`, `--landing-red`) still exist for hero sections but align with main palette.
